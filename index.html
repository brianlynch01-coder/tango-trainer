Getting started: Open this file in Edge or Chrome, download a D'Arienzo track (La Cumparsita or Dime Mi Amor) from YouTube as an MP3, and drag it onto the drop zone. The beat analysis runs in the background in a few seconds. Beat maps are cached so you only analyse once per track.

What to tap: The strong beat — roughly every two steps a dancer takes. In D'Arienzo this is the sharp downward punch of the bandoneon. Aim to land your tap on the sound, not after it.

Progression: D'Arienzo (~69 BPM) → Di Sarli (softer, cushioned in strings) → Pugliese (the beat breathes deliberately).
